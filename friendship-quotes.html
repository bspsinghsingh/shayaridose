English

Good friends are like stars — you don't always see them, but you know they're always there for you. 🌟

Hindi

English

Hindi

Hindi

English

The most beautiful discovery true friends make is that they can grow separately without growing apart. 🌱

Hindi

तेरी यारी में वो सुकून है जो किसी दौलत में नहीं। दोस्त, तू बेशकीमती है। 🌟

English

Real friends don't get offended when you insult them. They smile and come up with a better insult right back. 😂

Hindi

English

Hindi

Hindi

English

Friendship isn't about being inseparable — it's about being separated and knowing nothing will change. 💙

Hindi

English
